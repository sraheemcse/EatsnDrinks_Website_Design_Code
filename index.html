Our Heritage

The Rich History of Makhana (Fox Nuts)

Makhana (Fox Nuts), also known structurally as lotus seeds, has been celebrated for centuries across Eastern cultures as a sacred and deeply nourishing superfood. Cultivated primarily in clean wetland ecosystems, the process of harvesting and popping makhana seeds is a traditional art requiring exceptional patience and refinement.

At EATS N DRINKS, we bridge this ancient legacy with contemporary culinary experiences. By carefully sorting the highest quality seeds, we slow-roast and season them with premium ingredients—offering a snack that maintains its rich ancestral value while remaining highly appealing to international palates.
